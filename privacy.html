Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Bloom keeps everything on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, and no analytics. Your pregnancy information is never sent to us or anyone else — we have no way to see it.

The short version

  • All of your data is stored locally on your device, in your browser's private storage for this app.
  • We do not collect, transmit, sell, share, or have access to any of your information.
  • There is no account, no sign-in, and no user profile.
  • There is no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.
  • You can delete everything at any time by removing the app or clearing its site data.

What data the app stores (on your device)

To work, Bloom saves the information you enter — your pregnancy dates, your unit preference, and anything you log such as weights, measurements, feelings, symptoms, or notes. This is held only in your browser's on-device storage (IndexedDB) for this app. It stays on your phone or computer and is not copied anywhere by us.

What we collect

Nothing. Bloom has no backend server, no database we control, and no analytics. We cannot see your dates, your logs, or even whether you use the app.

Hosting and network access

The app's files are delivered from a static web host the first time you open or install it. Like any website, that host may automatically record standard technical request information (such as your IP address and the time of the request) in its own server logs; this is handled by the hosting provider, not by us, and is not linked to anything you enter in the app. Once Bloom is installed and its files are cached, it runs offline and makes no further network requests in normal use.

Cookies and tracking

Bloom does not use cookies, advertising identifiers, fingerprinting, or any tracking technologies. The on-device storage it uses is solely to save your own data so the app works.

Sharing your data

Because your data never leaves your device and we never receive it, there is nothing for us to share or sell, and we do not. Any backup, screenshot, or export you choose to create yourself is under your control — share it only with people you trust.

Deleting your data

Your data is removed when you delete the app from your home screen and clear its site data, or clear this site's data in your browser settings. Removal is permanent and cannot be undone by us, because we never held a copy.

Children

Bloom is intended for adults managing a pregnancy and is not directed to children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. Material changes to how the app handles data will be reflected in the app.

Contact

A contact method for privacy questions will be provided here when Bloom is made more widely available.
